KNOW ABOUT US


Established in the year 2013, We at D.D. ENTERPRISE are engaged in serving as manufacturers and expoters of various types of plastic Raw materials, Additives and Packaging materials. Depending upon the hi-tech machineries and diligent team of professionals we are able to produce Raw materials which are at par with international quality standards.


We at D.D. ENTERPRISE, have a vast experience of 25 years in 'Recyling' and 'Recprocessing' of Plastics. We always think of processing and using plastics in a 'Green' way with new techniqes and innovations.


We aim for the satisfaction of our clients to the best.
